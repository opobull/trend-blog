## 1. Schitt's Creek Arrives on HBO Max in Memory of Catherine O'Hara

![Entertainment Afternoon Roundup](/images/entertainment-afternoon-2026-02-07.jpg)

**HBO Max** will begin streaming the Emmy-winning comedy **Schitt's Creek** starting **Saturday, February 7th**, one week after the passing of beloved star **Catherine O'Hara** at age 71. The streaming platform is also launching a newly curated "Schitt's Creek playlist" via its in-app channels feature.

O'Hara's portrayal of the iconic **Moira Rose** earned her an Emmy Award and became one of her most celebrated roles in a decades-long career that included *Home Alone*, *Beetlejuice*, and *Best in Show*.

**Streaming Availability:**

| Platform | Status |
|----------|--------|
| HBO Max | Starting February 7, 2026 |
| Hulu | Currently Available |
| Prime Video | Currently Available |
| Netflix | 5 O'Hara films added recently |

The series, created by **Dan Levy** and **Eugene Levy**, ran for six seasons from 2015-2020 and swept the 2020 Emmy Awards with nine wins, including Outstanding Comedy Series.

## 2. Dark Winds Renewed for Season 5 Ahead of Season 4 Premiere

**AMC** has renewed the critically acclaimed crime drama **Dark Winds** for a fifth season, ahead of its Season 4 premiere on **February 15, 2026**. The Navajo Nation-set series, executive produced by **Robert Redford** and **George R.R. Martin**, has maintained a 100% Rotten Tomatoes score across its first three seasons.

**Zahn McClarnon** returns as Lt. Joe Leaphorn alongside **Kiowa Gordon** and **Jessica Matten**. Season 4 takes the characters from Navajo Nation to 1970s Los Angeles in search of a missing Navajo girl.

**Season 5 Details:**

| Item | Information |
|------|-------------|
| Network | AMC / AMC+ |
| Episodes | 8 |
| Filming Start | March 2026 |
| Location | Santa Fe, New Mexico |
| Expected Premiere | 2027 |

"Over four seasons, Dark Winds has masterfully blended compelling character-driven stories, Navajo culture, spirituality and breathtaking cinematography," said **Dan McDermott**, Chief Content Officer of AMC Networks.

**Season 4 Guest Stars:** Franka Potente (*Run Lola Run*), Chaske Spencer (*The Twilight Saga*), Titus Welliver (*Bosch*)

## 3. Predator Director Dan Trachtenberg Signs Three-Year Deal with Paramount

**Dan Trachtenberg**, who directed the acclaimed Predator trilogy (*Prey*, *Predator: Killer of Killers*, *Predator: Badlands*) for 20th Century Studios, has signed a three-year first-look deal with **Paramount Pictures**.

The deal marks a homecoming for Trachtenberg, who made his directorial debut with **10 Cloverfield Lane** (2016) at Paramount before moving to Disney's 20th Century Studios for the Predator franchise.

**Trachtenberg's Recent Films:**

| Title | Year | Platform |
|-------|------|----------|
| Prey | 2022 | Hulu |
| Predator: Killer of Killers | 2024 | Theaters |
| Predator: Badlands | 2025 | Theaters |

"Dan has an extraordinary ability to deliver films that are both daring and deeply entertaining," Paramount bosses **Josh Greenstein** and **Dana Goldberg** said in a joint statement.

Industry speculation suggests Trachtenberg could helm a major Paramount franchise, with **World War Z 2**, **Mission: Impossible**, **Transformers**, and **Star Trek** all mentioned as possibilities. His signing comes after Paramount's cash-infusing merger with Skydance.

## 4. Fast & Furious: Hollywood Drift Coaster Opens Summer 2026 at Universal Studios Hollywood

**Universal Studios Hollywood** has officially announced that **Fast & Furious: Hollywood Drift**, the park's first high-speed outdoor roller coaster, will open in **Summer 2026**. Franchise star **Vin Diesel** made the announcement in a television commercial set to air during the Winter Olympics, Super Bowl LX, and NBA All-Star Game.

**Coaster Specifications:**

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Top Speed | 72 mph (0 to 72 mph launch) |
| Track Length | 4,100 feet |
| Ride Vehicles | 360-degree rotating cars |
| Opening | Summer 2026 (after June 21) |

The coaster features state-of-the-art ride technology with first-of-its-kind innovations, including sound reduction technology for residential neighbors. Ride vehicles are themed to look like authentic Fast & Furious film cars.

The Hollywood Drift name references the 2006 film *The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift*. A Florida version with a 170-foot spike track will open at Universal Studios Florida in 2027.

## 5. Real Housewives of Rhode Island Sets April 2 Premiere on Bravo

## 6. FUZE Heist Thriller Acquired by Roadside Attractions & Saban Films

**Roadside Attractions** and **Saban Films** have acquired North American rights to **FUZE**, a heist thriller starring **Aaron Taylor-Johnson**, **Theo James**, **Gugu Mbatha-Raw**, and **Sam Worthington**. A major wide theatrical release is planned for spring 2026.

Directed by **David Mackenzie** (*Hell or High Water*, *Outlaw King*), the film unfolds in contemporary London when a WWII unexploded bomb is discovered, forcing a massive citywide evacuation that becomes cover for a meticulously planned heist.

**Film Details:**

| Item | Information |
|------|-------------|
| Director | David Mackenzie |
| Writer | Ben Hopkins |
| Cast | Aaron Taylor-Johnson, Theo James, Gugu Mbatha-Raw, Sam Worthington |
| UK Release | April 3, 2026 (Sky Cinema) |
| US Release | Spring 2026 (Theatrical) |

"With FUZE I really wanted to create something kinetic and cinematic – fusing the excitement of a heist movie with the nerve-racking tension of an unexploded bomb film," Mackenzie stated.

The film has already received critical acclaim, with Saban Films co-presidents **Shanan Becker** and **Jonathan Saba** calling it "exactly the kind of movie audiences are craving—a big-screen, action-packed blockbuster."
