## What Happened

The NHL announced on February 1, 2026, that Boston Bruins forward David Pastrnak has been named the NHL Second Star of the Month for January. The 29-year-old Czech forward dominated the stat sheet, ranking among the league leaders in multiple categories.

## January Statistics

| Category | Pastrnak's Numbers | NHL Rank |
|----------|-------------------|----------|
| Points | 25 | Tied 2nd |
| Assists | 20 | 2nd |
| Points per game | 1.79 | 2nd |
| Even strength points | 18 | 2nd |

Pastrnak recorded points in 12 of Boston's 14 games during January, including seven multi-point performances. The Bruins finished the month with an 11-2-1 record.

## Career Milestones in January

Pastrnak reached several significant milestones during the month:

- **January 10**: Career-high six-point game in a 10-2 win over the New York Rangers
- **January 17**: Played his 800th career NHL game against Chicago
- **January 26**: Recorded his 900th career point against the Rangers, becoming the second-fastest Bruin to reach that milestone

His 25 points in January were the most by a Bruins player in a single calendar month since Brad Marchand had 26 points in March 2018.

## Season Performance

Through 50 games this season, Pastrnak leads the Bruins in several categories:

| Stat | Total | NHL Rank |
|------|-------|----------|
| Points | 69 (22 G, 47 A) | 6th |
| Assists | 47 | Team leader |
| Even strength points | 44 | Team leader |
| Power play points | 25 | Team leader |

## Stadium Series Showdown

Pastrnak heads into the 2026 Navy Federal Credit Union NHL Stadium Series against the Tampa Bay Lightning at Raymond James Stadium on Sunday, February 1 (6:30 p.m. ET).

His teammates have praised his recent form. Defenseman Charlie McAvoy said: "If you don't know who David Pastrnak is, you are living under a rock."

Forward Fraser Minten described Pastrnak as "elite, elite" and added: "There are good players, there are great players and there are exceptional players, and I would put him in the highest tier you can."

## Career Numbers

Now in his 12th season with Boston, Pastrnak's career totals:

| Stat | Total |
|------|-------|
| Games | 806 |
| Goals | 413 |
| Assists | 489 |
| Points | 902 |
| Plus/Minus | +143 |

He ranks sixth on Boston's all-time points list and fourth in franchise history in multi-point games (257).

## What's Next

Pastrnak was named to Team Czechia's roster for the Olympic Winter Games Milano Cortina 2026, which runs February 11-22.
