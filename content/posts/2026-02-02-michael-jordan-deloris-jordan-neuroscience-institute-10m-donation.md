## What Happened

Novant Health New Hanover Regional Medical Center (NHRMC) in Wilmington, North Carolina, officially renamed its neuroscience institute the Deloris P. Jordan Neurosciences Institute on Saturday, February 1, 2026. The renaming follows Michael Jordan's $10 million donation in November 2025, made in honor of his mother.

## The Naming Ceremony

The Saturday morning ceremony brought together Novant Health representatives, community members, and the Jordan family to celebrate the historic naming.

Carl Armato, President and CEO of Novant Health, emphasized the organization's mission: "What I want you to know is that we at Novant Health are focused on delivering remarkable healthcare every day that really spans every community, city, and every generation."

Dr. Jefferey Beecher, director of neurosciences for NHRMC, expressed the significance of the honor: "I speak for all of neurosciences when I say how humbled we are to have our neuroscience institute named the Deloris P. Jordan Neurosciences Institute. We will strive to bring excellent service to our community and beyond."

## Deloris Jordan Named Global Ambassador

During the event, Deloris Jordan was recognized as Novant Health's Global Ambassador. She spoke about her passion for expanding healthcare access and knowledge worldwide.

"Everybody should have an opportunity for good health and education," Deloris Jordan said. "How do you make it? Coming together and understanding one another. Your skin may be different, but your blood is still red."

## What the Donation Supports

The $10 million donation will enable continued research into brain health at the Wilmington facility. Novant Health representatives highlighted how the partnership will drive positive growth in neuroscience research and community healthcare.

The timing of the building dedication was noted as especially meaningful, coming just one day before the neuroscience institute's third anniversary.
