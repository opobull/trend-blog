## What Happened

Lionsgate and Universal have released a new poster for *Michael*, Antoine Fuqua's upcoming biopic about the King of Pop. The poster was unveiled ahead of the 2026 Grammy Awards on February 1st.

The new image features Jaafar Jackson — Michael Jackson's nephew and son of Jermaine Jackson — dressed in the iconic red leather jacket and sunglasses from Michael's legendary "Beat It" music video.

## Film Details

### Production Team

| Role | Name |
|------|------|
| Director | Antoine Fuqua (*Training Day*, *The Equalizer* trilogy) |
| Screenwriter | John Logan (Oscar nominee for *Gladiator*, *Skyfall*) |
| Producer | Graham King (*Bohemian Rhapsody*) |

### Cast

- **Jaafar Jackson** as Michael Jackson
- **Miles Teller** as John Branca
- **Colman Domingo** in supporting role
- **Nia Long** in supporting role
- **Laura Harrier** in supporting role
- **Juliano Krue Valdi** in supporting role

## Official Synopsis

> "Michael is the cinematic portrayal of the life and legacy of one of the most influential artists the world has ever known. The film tells the story of Michael Jackson's life beyond the music, tracing his journey from the discovery of his extraordinary talent as the lead of the Jackson Five, to the visionary artist whose creative ambition fueled a relentless pursuit to become the biggest entertainer in the world."

The film promises to highlight both Jackson's life off-stage and some of his most iconic performances from his early solo career.

## Production Background

Producer Graham King previously helmed *Bohemian Rhapsody*, the Freddie Mercury biopic that grossed nearly $1 billion worldwide and won several Academy Awards including Best Actor for Rami Malek.

## What's Next

*Michael* is scheduled for premiere in April 2026. More promotional materials, including a potential new trailer, may be released in the coming weeks.
