The Star Wars saga spans nearly 50 years of storytelling across 12 films, 10+ TV series, and thousands of years of in-universe history. Whether you're a newcomer or rewatching the entire saga, this guide will help you watch Star Wars in **chronological timeline order** - the order events actually happened in the galaxy far, far away.

**Last Updated**: February 2026 | Includes all Disney+ series through 2026

---

## Release Order vs Chronological Order: Which Is Better?

Before diving in, you need to decide:

- **Release Order**: Watch in the order George Lucas and Disney released them (1977 first). Best for first-timers — experience the saga as the world did, with twists intact.
- **Chronological Order**: Watch in the order events happened in the Star Wars universe. Best for rewatches — reveals hidden connections across the saga.

**Our recommendation**: First-time viewers should use **release order**. This guide is for everyone else.

## Complete Star Wars Chronological Watch Order (2026)

### **Era 1: The High Republic (Hundreds of Years Before the Prequels)**

#### 1. **The Acolyte** (Disney+ Series, 2024)
Set 100 years before *The Phantom Menace*, during the golden age of the Jedi Order. A mysterious former Padawan reunites with her Jedi Master to investigate a string of murders. Dark secrets from the past emerge that challenge everything the Jedi believe.

**Episodes**: 8 | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Why watch**: Shows the Jedi at their most powerful — and their most complacent. The prequel to all prequels.

---

### **Era 2: The Prequel Trilogy (32-19 BBY)**

#### 2. **Episode I: The Phantom Menace** (1999)
The Trade Federation blockades Naboo, forcing young Queen Amidala to seek help. Jedi Knights Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi discover a young slave named Anakin Skywalker who is extraordinarily strong in the Force.

**Runtime**: 136 min | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Key events**: Introduction of Anakin Skywalker, Darth Maul, midichlorians, Jar Jar Binks

---

#### 3. **Episode II: Attack of the Clones** (2002)
Ten years later, Anakin is now a Jedi Padawan. An assassination attempt on Senator Amidala leads Obi-Wan to discover a secret clone army — and Anakin begins his forbidden romance with Padmé.

**Runtime**: 142 min | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Key events**: Clone Wars begin, Anakin and Padmé's marriage, Jango Fett, Count Dooku

---

#### 4. **Tales of the Jedi** (Disney+ Animated, 2022-2024)
Short animated stories that expand the prequel era. Follows young Ahsoka Tano's origins and Count Dooku's fall to the dark side.

**Episodes**: 6 (Season 1) + more | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Best for**: Prequel fans who want deeper lore on Ahsoka and Dooku.

---

#### 5. **The Clone Wars** (Disney+ Animated, 2008-2020)
The definitive Clone Wars story. Follows Anakin Skywalker, Obi-Wan Kenobi, and Ahsoka Tano through hundreds of battles and storylines during the war between Episodes II and III.

**Episodes**: 133 across 7 seasons | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Essential seasons**: 1-2 for context, 5-7 for the payoff. Season 7's final arc runs parallel to Episode III.

**Why watch**: Transforms the prequel era completely. Ahsoka becomes one of Star Wars' greatest characters here.

---

#### 6. **Episode III: Revenge of the Sith** (2005)
The fall of Anakin Skywalker. The Clone Wars end as Anakin is seduced to the dark side, Palpatine executes Order 66, and Darth Vader is born.

**Runtime**: 140 min | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Key events**: Order 66, birth of Darth Vader, birth of Luke and Leia, the Jedi Purge begins

---

### **Era 3: The Dark Times (19-0 BBY)**

#### 7. **Solo: A Star Wars Story** (2018)
Han Solo's origin story. A young Han meets Chewbacca, Lando Calrissian, and makes the Kessel Run in 12 parsecs. Set during the early days of the Empire.

**Runtime**: 135 min | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Key events**: Han meets Chewie, the Millennium Falcon's origins, Maul's return

---

#### 8. **The Bad Batch** (Disney+ Animated, 2021-2024)
Immediately after Order 66, Clone Force 99 — a squad of genetically enhanced clone troopers — go rogue rather than serve the new Empire. Three seasons following their survival and found family.

**Episodes**: 47 across 3 seasons | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Why watch**: Best animated Star Wars since Clone Wars. Shows how the galaxy transforms from Republic to Empire.

---

#### 9. **Obi-Wan Kenobi** (Disney+ Series, 2022)
Set 10 years after Revenge of the Sith. Obi-Wan is in exile on Tatooine when a young Princess Leia is kidnapped, forcing him out of hiding. Their journey leads to a confrontation with Darth Vader.

**Episodes**: 6 | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Key events**: Obi-Wan and Vader's rematch, Leia's early years, reveals about Order 66 survivors

---

#### 10. **Andor** (Disney+ Series, 2022-2025)
The grittiest, most realistic Star Wars ever made. Cassian Andor's radicalization into a rebel spy, told over two seasons covering the 5 years before *Rogue One*.

**Episodes**: 24 across 2 seasons | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Why watch**: The most critically acclaimed live-action Star Wars content ever made. A political thriller set in the Star Wars universe. Rotten Tomatoes: 96%.

---

#### 11. **Rebels** (Disney+ Animated, 2014-2018)
Follows a crew of rebels 14 years before *A New Hope*, as they fight the Empire and help ignite the larger Rebellion. Bridges the prequel era to the original trilogy beautifully.

**Episodes**: 75 across 4 seasons | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Why watch**: Essential for understanding Ahsoka, Ezra Bridger, and the Rebellion's origins.

---

#### 12. **Rogue One: A Star Wars Story** (2016)
The Rebellion steals the Death Star plans, setting up *A New Hope* directly. Takes place days before Episode IV.

**Runtime**: 133 min | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Key events**: The Scarif battle, Death Star weakness explanation, iconic Darth Vader hallway scene

---

### **Era 4: The Original Trilogy (0-4 ABY)**

#### 13. **Episode IV: A New Hope** (1977)
The beginning. Farm boy Luke Skywalker joins the Rebel Alliance, meets Han Solo and Obi-Wan Kenobi, and helps destroy the Death Star.

**Runtime**: 121 min | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Key events**: Introduction of Luke, Leia, Han, Vader, the Force, lightsabers. The movie that started it all.

---

#### 14. **Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back** (1980)
Widely considered the greatest Star Wars film. The Empire strikes back hard. Luke trains with Yoda. Han is captured. And Vader reveals the truth about Luke's father.

**Runtime**: 124 min | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Key events**: "I am your father," Yoda introduced, Han frozen in carbonite, Battle of Hoth

---

#### 15. **Episode VI: Return of the Jedi** (1983)
The saga's original conclusion. Luke faces his destiny, Han is rescued, and the Empire falls at the Battle of Endor.

**Runtime**: 131 min | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Key events**: Jabba the Hutt, Emperor Palpatine, Anakin Skywalker's redemption

---

### **Era 5: The New Republic (9-28 ABY)**

#### 16. **The Mandalorian** (Disney+ Series, 2019-present)
Set 5 years after *Return of the Jedi*. A lone Mandalorian bounty hunter is hired to retrieve a mysterious asset — who turns out to be Grogu (Baby Yoda), a Force-sensitive child. This show revitalized Star Wars fandom.

**Seasons**: 3 complete + Season 4 in production | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Why watch**: The show that proved Star Wars could work on TV. Grogu is the most beloved new Star Wars character in decades.

---

#### 17. **The Book of Boba Fett** (Disney+ Series, 2022)
Boba Fett returns to Tatooine to claim Jabba's criminal empire. Spins out of The Mandalorian and connects back to it.

**Episodes**: 7 | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Note**: Episodes 5-6 are essentially Mandalorian Season 2.5 — watch before Mandalorian Season 3.

---

#### 18. **Ahsoka** (Disney+ Series, 2023-present)
Ahsoka Tano hunts for the missing Grand Admiral Thrawn and connects to characters from *Rebels*. Effectively a live-action continuation of the animated series.

**Episodes**: 8 (Season 1) | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Why watch**: Essential if you watched Rebels. Brings beloved animated characters into live-action.

---

#### 19. **Skeleton Crew** (Disney+ Series, 2024-present)
Four kids discover an uncharted planet and accidentally board a ship crewed by a peculiar stranger, Jod Na Nawood. A coming-of-age adventure set in the New Republic era.

**Episodes**: 8 (Season 1) | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Why watch**: The most family-friendly live-action Star Wars. Like *The Goonies* in space.

---

#### 20. **The Mandalorian & Grogu** (Film, 2026)
The theatrical conclusion to Din Djarin and Grogu's story. Currently in production for a 2026 release.

**Where to watch**: Theaters (2026)

---

### **Era 6: The Sequel Trilogy (34-35 ABY)**

#### 21. **Episode VII: The Force Awakens** (2015)
30 years after the Empire's defeat. A scavenger named Rey discovers the Force, joins the Resistance, and encounters a new villain: Kylo Ren.

**Runtime**: 138 min | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Key events**: Introduction of Rey, Finn, Poe, Kylo Ren. Han Solo's fate.

---

#### 22. **Episode VIII: The Last Jedi** (2017)
The most divisive Star Wars film. The Resistance flees the First Order while Rey seeks training from a broken Luke Skywalker.

**Runtime**: 152 min | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Key events**: Luke's arc, Holdo maneuver, the throne room battle

---

#### 23. **Episode IX: The Rise of Skywalker** (2019)
The saga's (controversial) finale. Palpatine returns, the battle for the galaxy concludes, and the Skywalker saga ends.

**Runtime**: 142 min | **Where to watch**: Disney+

**Key events**: Palpatine's return, Rey's true identity, the final battle

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Should I watch in release order or chronological order?

**First-time viewers**: Release order. Start with *A New Hope* (1977). The major twists (especially from Empire Strikes Back) hit much harder.

**Rewatchers and fans**: Chronological is a great experience that reveals new connections.

### Do I have to watch the animated shows?

No — but **The Clone Wars** significantly improves your understanding of Anakin's fall in *Revenge of the Sith*, and **Andor** is arguably the best thing in the franchise since *The Empire Strikes Back*.

### Is the High Republic era essential?

**The Acolyte** is set during the High Republic but stands alone. It's not required viewing but does add context to how the Jedi became what they are in the prequels.

### Where does The Mandalorian fit?

Chronologically after *Return of the Jedi*, set in 9 ABY. Watch it after the original trilogy and before the sequel trilogy.

### Is Rogue One necessary before A New Hope?

Technically no (billions of people watched them out of order) — but watching Rogue One immediately before A New Hope is one of the most emotional Star Wars viewing experiences possible.

### What about the Clone Wars movie (2008)?

It's a theatrical film that serves as a pilot for the animated series. Watch it before Clone Wars Season 1 or skip it (the show quickly improves beyond the movie).
