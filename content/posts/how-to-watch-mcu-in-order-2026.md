The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) has grown into a massive franchise spanning over 30 films and dozens of TV shows. Whether you're a newcomer or rewatching the entire saga, this guide will help you watch the MCU in **chronological order** - the order events actually happened in the Marvel timeline, not release order.

**Last Updated**: February 2026 | Includes all Phase 5 releases

---

## Why Watch in Chronological Order?

Watching the MCU chronologically offers a unique perspective:
- **Experience the timeline as it unfolds** - from World War II to the multiverse saga
- **Better understand character arcs** - see how events connect across decades
- **Catch hidden connections** - references and Easter eggs make more sense
- **Perfect for rewatches** - offers a fresh viewing experience

## Complete MCU Chronological Watch Order (2026)

### **Phase 1: The Beginning (1940s - 2012)**

#### 1. **Captain America: The First Avenger** (1942-1945)
The MCU's chronological starting point. Follow Steve Rogers as he becomes Captain America during World War II and battles the Red Skull.

**Where to watch**: Disney+

#### 2. **Captain Marvel** (1995)
Set in the 1990s, Carol Danvers discovers her powers and uncovers the truth about the Kree-Skrull war.

**Where to watch**: Disney+

#### 3. **Iron Man** (2008)
Tony Stark builds the first Iron Man suit and launches the modern MCU era.

**Where to watch**: Disney+

#### 4. **Iron Man 2** (2010)
Tony faces new enemies while dealing with palladium poisoning from his arc reactor.

**Where to watch**: Disney+

#### 5. **The Incredible Hulk** (2010)
Bruce Banner battles the Abomination while searching for a cure. (Takes place during "Fury's Big Week")

**Where to watch**: Disney+

#### 6. **Thor** (2011)
The God of Thunder is banished to Earth and must prove himself worthy.

**Where to watch**: Disney+

#### 7. **The Avengers** (2012)
**The Battle of New York** - Earth's Mightiest Heroes assemble for the first time to stop Loki's invasion.

**Where to watch**: Disney+

## FAQ

### How long does it take to watch the entire MCU in chronological order?

Approximately **185+ hours** (7.7 days) including all films and Disney+ shows as of February 2026.

### Should I watch in chronological or release order?

**Release order** for first-time viewers. **Chronological order** for rewatches or if you want to experience the timeline as it unfolds.

### Are the TV shows required viewing?

The Disney+ shows (WandaVision, Loki, etc.) are now essential. Older Netflix shows are optional but Daredevil is becoming more relevant.

### What's the difference between the Infinity Saga and Multiverse Saga?

**Infinity Saga**: Phases 1-3, focuses on the Infinity Stones and Thanos.
**Multiverse Saga**: Phases 4-6, explores alternate realities and Kang the Conqueror.
