## 1. Young Sherlock - Guy Ritchie's Prime Video Series Gets Full Trailer

![Young Sherlock Prime Video](/images/entertainment-am-2026-02-06-2-1.jpg)

Prime Video has released the full official trailer for **Young Sherlock**, a new eight-episode series from director Guy Ritchie. The show stars **Hero Fiennes Tiffin** as the young legendary detective and will premiere on **March 4, 2026** with all episodes available at once.

Described as an "explosive re-imagining of the iconic character's early days," the series serves as a spin-off from Ritchie's Robert Downey Jr.-led **Sherlock Holmes** films (2009, 2011). The show follows a teenage Sherlock who gets framed for murder and must clear his name using his nascent deductive abilities.

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Platform | Prime Video |
| Release Date | March 4, 2026 |
| Episodes | 8 (all at once) |
| Director | Guy Ritchie |
| Lead | Hero Fiennes Tiffin |

**Cast:** Hero Fiennes Tiffin (Sherlock Holmes), Colin Firth, Joseph Fiennes, Natascha McElhone

The series is based on Andrew Lane's "Young Sherlock Holmes" book series. The trailer showcases Ritchie's signature fast-paced, stylized action sequences combined with mystery-solving elements that made his previous Sherlock films so popular.

## 2. The Dinosaurs - Steven Spielberg's Netflix Documentary Reveals Morgan Freeman Narration

The documentary promises to combine cutting-edge CGI with scientific accuracy to bring prehistoric creatures to life in unprecedented detail.

## 4. Exit 8 - Japanese Time Loop Horror Gets US Trailer from NEON

![Exit 8 Japanese Horror Film](/images/entertainment-am-2026-02-06-2-3.jpg)

NEON has released the official US trailer for **Exit 8**, a Japanese psychological horror thriller based on the viral video game of the same name. Directed by **Genki Kawamura** and starring **Kazunari Ninomiya**, the film arrives in US theaters on **April 10, 2026**.

The movie follows a man trapped in an endless, sterile subway passageway who must find Exit 8. The rules are simple: spot the anomalies to progress, or remain stuck in the maddening loop forever. Miss an anomaly, and you return to the beginning.

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| US Release Date | April 10, 2026 |
| Distributor | NEON |
| Director | Genki Kawamura |
| Lead | Kazunari Ninomiya |
| Based On | "The Exit 8" video game |

**Plot:** "A man trapped in an endless sterile subway passageway sets out to find Exit 8. The rules are displayed on a sign on the wall next to the player, with the player starting at Exit 0."

The trailer marries the nightmare logic of "The Shining" with elements of The Backrooms internet phenomenon. The film premiered at the 2025 Cannes Film Festival in the Midnight section and has been generating buzz at genre festivals worldwide. The original video game by KOTAKE CREATE became a viral sensation for its creepy atmosphere and spot-the-difference gameplay mechanics.

## 6. Search Party - All 5 Seasons of HBO Max Hit Comedy Join Netflix

![Search Party HBO Max Netflix](/images/entertainment-am-2026-02-06-2-6.jpg)

Netflix has added all five seasons (50 episodes) of **Search Party**, the critically acclaimed dark comedy that originally aired on HBO Max. The complete series became available for streaming on **February 5, 2026**.

Created by Sarah-Violet Bliss and Charles Rogers, the series stars **Alia Shawkat** (Arrested Development) and follows a group of self-absorbed twenty-somethings who become entangled in a missing persons investigation that spirals into increasingly dark territory.

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Platform | Netflix |
| Available | February 5, 2026 |
| Seasons | 5 (50 episodes) |
| Original Network | HBO Max |
| Rotten Tomatoes | 96% |

**Cast:** Alia Shawkat, John Reynolds, John Early, Meredith Hagner, Brandon Micheal Hall

The series ran from 2016 to 2022 and evolved from a dark comedy mystery into increasingly surreal territory, including a cult subplot and a satirical take on cancel culture. With its 96% Rotten Tomatoes score, Search Party has been called one of the most underrated comedies of the 2010s and 2020s.

The addition comes as part of Netflix's ongoing licensing partnership with Warner Bros. Television and HBO/HBO Max.
