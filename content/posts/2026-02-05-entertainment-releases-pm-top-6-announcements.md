## 1. Lucky - Anya Taylor-Joy Returns to Series TV on Apple TV+

![Lucky Apple TV Series](/images/entertainment-pm-2026-02-05-1.jpg)

Apple TV+ has unveiled the teaser trailer and premiere date for **Lucky**, marking Anya Taylor-Joy's first major TV series since *The Queen's Gambit*. The seven-part action drama will premiere on **July 15, 2026**, with new episodes releasing weekly through August 19.

Based on Marissa Stapley's bestselling novel, *Lucky* follows a con artist on the run after a heist goes terribly wrong. The first teaser reveals intense action sequences with screaming, wild car chases, bloodshed, fire, and steamy moments between Taylor-Joy and co-star Drew Starkey.

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Premiere | July 15, 2026 |
| Episodes | 7 (weekly release) |
| Platform | Apple TV+ |
| Source Material | Lucky by Marissa Stapley |

**Cast:** Anya Taylor-Joy, Drew Starkey, Aunjanue Ellis Taylor, Annette Bening, Timothy Olyphant, Clifton Collins Jr.

Taylor-Joy sports a platinum blonde bob for the role — her shortest hairstyle in almost 10 years.

## 2. Cross Season 2 - Aldis Hodge Returns as Alex Cross on Prime Video

![Cross Season 2 Prime Video](/images/entertainment-pm-2026-02-05-2.jpg)

**Cross Season 2** premieres on Prime Video on **February 11, 2026**, with the first three episodes dropping simultaneously. New episodes will be released weekly until March 18.

Based on James Patterson's bestselling Alex Cross novels, the series stars Aldis Hodge as the brilliant detective and forensic psychologist. Season 2 sees Cross hunting a ruthless female vigilante who targets corrupt billionaires — a case that becomes deeply personal and dangerous, forcing him to protect a new target.

**Key Information:**

|------|---------|
| Premiere | February 11, 2026 |
| Episodes | Weekly until March 18 |
| Platform | Prime Video |
| Showrunner | Ben Watkins |

**Cast:** Aldis Hodge, Matthew Lillard, Isaiah Mustafa, Juanita Jennings, Alona Tal, Samantha Walkes, Caleb Elijah, Melody Hurd, Jennifer Wigmore, Eloise Mumford, Ryan Eggold, Jeanine Mason, Wes Chatham, Johnny Ray Gill

Matthew Lillard joins the cast for Season 2, bringing Scooby-Doo icon status to this thrilling crime drama.

## 3. BAKI-DOU: The Invincible Samurai - English Dub Trailer & Cast Revealed

![BAKI-DOU The Invincible Samurai](/images/entertainment-pm-2026-02-05-3.jpg)

The series brings legendary swordsman Miyamoto Musashi back from the dead to face Baki's toughest fighters. Following the conclusion of the greatest father-son fight in the franchise, this "battle of eras" will determine whether the legendary samurai or modern martial artists are truly the strongest.

## 4. The Bluff - Priyanka Chopra Stars in R-Rated Pirate Thriller

![The Bluff Priyanka Chopra](/images/entertainment-pm-2026-02-05-4.jpg)

Set in the 19th-century Caribbean, the film follows a former female pirate who must protect her family when the sins of her past catch up to her. The action-packed thriller promises high-stakes drama with stunning coastal settings.

## 5. Last Ride - Spike Lee-Produced Survival Thriller Coming February 20

![Last Ride Movie](/images/entertainment-pm-2026-02-05-5.jpg)

**Last Ride**, a gripping survival thriller executive produced by Spike Lee, will release on digital platforms on **February 20, 2026** via Quiver Distribution.

Set in the winter of 1982, the film follows three American boys who find themselves stranded in a cable car with a dead body, suspended midair in the mountains of Norway during a rare celestial event. As tensions rise and the temperature drops, the boys must confront both the elements and dark secrets.

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Release Date | February 20, 2026 |
| Platform | Digital (VOD) |
| Distributor | Quiver Distribution |
| Executive Producers | Spike Lee, Todd Slater |

**Cast:** Roman Griffin Davis (Jojo Rabbit), Felix Jamieson, Charlie Price, Kristofer Hivju (Game of Thrones)

Roman Griffin Davis, who won hearts in *Jojo Rabbit*, takes the lead in this claustrophobic thriller that combines survival horror with coming-of-age drama.

## 6. The Hermit - Lou Ferrigno's Horror Debut as Cannibal Villain

![The Hermit Lou Ferrigno](/images/entertainment-pm-2026-02-05-6.jpg)

**The Hermit** marks Lou Ferrigno's first-ever horror role, with the original Incredible Hulk star playing a terrifying cannibal pig farmer. The film debuts on digital and on-demand platforms on **March 3, 2026** via Uncork'd Entertainment.

The brutal horror-thriller follows teenagers Lisa and Eric, who are dragged on a vacation into the woods where they encounter an unstoppable cannibal pig farmer who sells jerky made from human flesh. Ferrigno transforms from superhero icon to nightmare fuel in this dark, twisted tale.

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Release Date | March 3, 2026 |
| Platform | Digital & VOD |
| Distributor | Uncork'd Entertainment |
| Genre | Horror/Thriller |

**Cast:** Lou Ferrigno (The Incredible Hulk), Malina Weissman (A Series of Unfortunate Events), Anthony Turpel (Love, Victor)

This marks a dramatic departure for Ferrigno, who has spent decades as a superhero icon but now embraces his villainous side in this gruesome horror film.
