## What Happened

Lola Young took the stage at the 2026 Grammy Awards on Sunday, February 1, in her first major performance since collapsing during a concert in September 2025. The 25-year-old British singer-songwriter delivered her comeback performance as part of a special Best New Artist segment.

## The Health Scare

On September 27, 2025, Young collapsed mid-performance at the All Things Go Festival in New York City. The incident quickly went viral on social media.

The collapse was attributed to:
- Exhaustion
- Dehydration
- Ongoing mental health struggles

Three days after the incident, Young announced she would be taking a break from touring to focus on her recovery.

She reassured fans that she is "back and feeling better than ever."

## Grammy Night Performance

The 2026 Grammys marked another milestone in Young's recovery. She performed as part of a special segment featuring all eight Best New Artist nominees together.

## About Lola Young

Young is a British singer-songwriter known for her raw, emotionally honest music. Her songs "Messy" and "Wish You Were Dead" have gained significant attention for their candid approach to relationships and personal struggles.

Her Grammy nomination for Best New Artist represents recognition of her rising profile in the music industry, making her health recovery and comeback performance particularly meaningful for her career trajectory.
