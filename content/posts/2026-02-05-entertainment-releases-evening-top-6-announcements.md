## 1. War Machine - Alan Ritchson Battles Alien Threat in Netflix Sci-Fi Action Film

![War Machine Netflix](/images/entertainment-evening-2026-02-05-1.jpg)

Netflix has dropped the official trailer for **War Machine**, a new sci-fi action thriller starring Alan Ritchson. The film will premiere on **March 6, 2026** on the streaming platform.

The movie follows a team of U.S. Army Rangers recruits who stumble upon an unparalleled danger during a training exercise. "Whatever that thing is, it's hunting us, and it won't stop," Ritchson says in the footage. "It's not about us anymore. It's about warning everybody that thing's coming."

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Release Date | March 6, 2026 |
| Platform | Netflix |
| Director | Patrick Hughes |
| Genre | Sci-Fi Action Thriller |

**Cast:** Alan Ritchson, Dennis Quaid, Stephan James, Jai Courtney, Esai Morales, Blake Richardson, Keiynan Lonsdale, Daniel Webber

Director Patrick Hughes (The Hitman's Bodyguard, The Man From Toronto) helmed the film from a script he co-wrote with James Beaufort. Ritchson, known for his titular role in Amazon's Reacher series, continues to cement his status as an action star.

## 3. Michael - Michael Jackson Biopic Trailer Gets Super Bowl Premiere

![Michael Jackson Biopic](/images/entertainment-evening-2026-02-05-3.jpg)

## 6. Happy's Place - Reba McEntire Sitcom Renewed for Season 3 on NBC

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Status | Renewed for Season 3 |
| Network | NBC |
