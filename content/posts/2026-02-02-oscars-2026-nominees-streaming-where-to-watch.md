## The 98th Academy Awards Are Coming

The 2026 Oscars ceremony is set for **Sunday, March 15 at 4:00 PM PST**. With less than six weeks until Hollywood's biggest night, viewers are scrambling to catch up on the nominated films.

Here's where you can stream or watch every major Oscar nominee.

## Best Picture Nominees: Streaming Availability

### Sinners (Ryan Coogler)

**Nominations:** Best Picture, Actor in a Leading Role, Actress in a Leading Role, Actress in a Supporting Role, Actor in a Supporting Role, Directing, Cinematography, Writing (Original & Adapted Screenplay), Film Editing, Music (Original Song and Score), Sound, Production Design, Visual Effects, Costume Design, Makeup and Hairstyling

**Where to Watch:** Fandango at Home, HBO Max, Prime Video, Hulu

The frontrunner with the most nominations of the night.

### Marty Supreme (Josh Safdie)

**Nominations:** Best Picture, Actor in a Leading Role, Directing, Cinematography, Writing (Original Screenplay), Film Editing, Production Design, Costume Design

**Where to Watch:** Now in Theaters

### Frankenstein (Guillermo del Toro)

**Nominations:** Best Picture, Actor in a Supporting Role, Cinematography, Writing (Adapted Screenplay), Film Editing, Music (Original Score), Sound, Production Design, Costume Design, Makeup and Hairstyling

**Where to Watch:** Netflix

### Hamnet

**Nominations:** Best Picture, Actress in a Leading Role, Directing, Writing (Adapted Screenplay), Music, Production Design, Costume Design

**Where to Watch:** Now in Theaters

### One Battle After Another

**Nominations:** Best Picture, Actor in a Leading Role, Actress in a Supporting Role, Actor in a Supporting Role, Directing, Cinematography, Writing (Adapted Screenplay), Film Editing, Music (Original Score), Sound, Production Design

**Where to Watch:** Fandango at Home, HBO Max, Hulu, Prime Video

### Sentimental Value

**Nominations:** Best Picture, Actress in a Leading Role, Actress in a Supporting Role, Actor in a Supporting Role, International Feature Film, Directing, Cinematography, Writing (Original Screenplay), Film Editing

**Where to Watch:** Fandango at Home

### F1

**Nominations:** Best Picture

**Where to Watch:** Fandango at Home, Apple TV+, Prime Video

### Bugonia

**Nominations:** Best Picture, Actress in a Leading Role, Writing (Adapted Screenplay), Music (Original Score)

**Where to Watch:** Fandango at Home, Peacock, Prime Video

### Train Dreams

**Nominations:** Best Picture, Writing (Adapted Screenplay), Music (Original Song)

**Where to Watch:** Netflix

### The Secret Agent

**Nominations:** Best Picture, Actor in a Leading Role, International Feature Film

**Where to Watch:** In Theaters, Apple TV+
