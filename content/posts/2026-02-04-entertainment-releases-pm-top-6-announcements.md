## 1. Scream 7 Super Bowl Trailer Released with First-Ever IMAX Release

![Scream 7 Movie](/images/entertainment-pm-2026-02-04-1.jpg)

Paramount Pictures has released a special Super Bowl trailer for **Scream 7**, marking a historic moment for the franchise with its first-ever IMAX release. The footage centers on Neve Campbell's Sidney Prescott, who must protect her daughter from a new Ghostface killer.

"When a new Ghostface killer emerges in the quiet town where Sidney Prescott has built a new life, her darkest fears are realized as her daughter becomes the next target," reads the official logline. The trailer reveals that this Ghostface has been planning the attack for years, with Sidney's daughter Tatum now the same age Sidney was during the original Woodsboro murders.

**Cast:** Neve Campbell, Isabel May, Courteney Cox, Jasmin Savoy Brown, Mason Gooding, Matthew Lillard (returning as Stu Macher), Joel McHale, Mckenna Grace, Anna Camp, David Arquette, and Scott Foley

**Release Date:** February 27, 2026 (Theaters + IMAX)

**Director:** Kevin Williamson (original Scream screenwriter)

## 2. In the Blink of an Eye - Official Hulu Trailer Released

![In the Blink of an Eye Hulu](/images/entertainment-pm-2026-02-04-2.jpg)

Hulu and Searchlight Pictures have released the official trailer for **In the Blink of an Eye**, a sci-fi film from Academy Award-winning director Andrew Stanton (Finding Nemo, WALL-E, Finding Dory). The film premiered at Sundance Film Festival 2026 where it won the Alfred P. Sloan Prize.

The movie stars Kate McKinnon, Rashida Jones, and Daveed Diggs in a story exploring the connections shared across time and human experience. This marks Stanton's second live-action feature film following 2012's John Carter.

Stanton is currently directing Pixar's highly anticipated Toy Story 5, scheduled for theatrical release this June.

**Cast:** Kate McKinnon, Rashida Jones, Daveed Diggs

**Release Date:** February 27, 2026 (Hulu / Hulu on Disney+)

**Director:** Andrew Stanton

## 3. The Kelly Clarkson Show Ending After Seven Seasons

![Kelly Clarkson Show](/images/entertainment-pm-2026-02-04-3.jpg)

Kelly Clarkson has announced that **The Kelly Clarkson Show** will conclude after its current seventh season. The announcement came on February 2, 2026, with the host citing a desire to "prioritize my kids" in her decision.

"This was not an easy decision, but this season will be my last hosting The Kelly Clarkson Show. Stepping away from the daily schedule will allow me to prioritize my kids, which feels necessary and right for this next chapter of our lives," Clarkson wrote in her statement.

The show will continue airing through Fall 2026 with special guest hosts. The decision comes months after the death of Clarkson's ex-husband, talent manager Brandon Blackstock, in August 2025 due to cancer. Clarkson shares daughter River Rose (11) and son Remington Alexander (9) with her late ex-husband.

**Show Achievements:**
- 24 Daytime Emmy Awards
- 4 consecutive wins for Outstanding Daytime Talk Series
- 4 consecutive wins for Outstanding Talk Show Host
- Famous "Kellyoke" segment became a cultural staple

Clarkson confirmed she will continue releasing music and appearing as a judge on The Voice.

**Final Episodes:** Through Fall 2026

## 4. Daredevil: Born Again Season 2 Premiere Date Confirmed

![Daredevil Born Again Season 2](/images/entertainment-pm-2026-02-04-4.jpg)

Marvel Studios has confirmed that **Daredevil: Born Again Season 2** will premiere on Disney+ on **March 24, 2026**. New promo art has been revealed featuring the Man Without Fear and the shadow of Mayor Wilson Fisk looming over Hell's Kitchen.

Season 2 will find Matt Murdock in a "completely different reality" as he "fights the power in a massive way," according to Marvel Television boss Brad Winderbaum. The season focuses on the trappings of power and how difficult it is for Kingpin to maintain control.

The season also marks the long-awaited return of Krysten Ritter as Jessica Jones, with Winderbaum teasing "a lot of very cool surprises" about what the character has been up to since we last saw her.

In related news, Season 3 filming is scheduled to begin on March 6, 2026.

**Cast:** Charlie Cox, Vincent D'Onofrio, Krysten Ritter, Deborah Ann Woll, Wilson Bethel, Ayelet Zurer, Margarita Levieva, Matthew Lillard

**Premiere Date:** March 24, 2026 (Disney+)

**Episodes:** 8

## 5. Lucky - Apple TV Series Teaser and Premiere Date Revealed

![Lucky Apple TV Series](/images/entertainment-pm-2026-02-04-5.jpg)

Apple TV has unveiled the teaser trailer and premiere date for **Lucky**, a new thriller series starring Anya Taylor-Joy. The first two episodes will debut on **July 15, 2026**, with new episodes arriving every Wednesday through August 19.

Based on the book by Marissa Stapley, the series follows Taylor-Joy as a con artist named Lucky who is forced to go on the run when a multi-million-dollar heist goes sideways. Pursued by both the FBI and a ruthless crime boss played by Annette Bening, Lucky must fight for her life and a way out.

The announcement was made at Apple TV's 2026 press day on February 3.

**Cast:** Anya Taylor-Joy, Drew Starkey, Annette Bening, Timothy Olyphant, Aunjanue Ellis-Taylor, Clifton Collins Jr., William Fichtner

**Executive Producers:** Jonathan Tropper, Cassie Pappas, Reese Witherspoon, Lauren Neustadter (Hello Sunshine), Anya Taylor-Joy

**Premiere Date:** July 15, 2026 (Apple TV+)

## 6. How to Get to Heaven from Belfast - Netflix Comedy-Thriller Premieres February 12

![How to Get to Heaven from Belfast](/images/entertainment-pm-2026-02-04-6.jpg)

From the creator of **Derry Girls**, Lisa McGee returns with a new comedy-thriller series on Netflix. **How to Get to Heaven from Belfast** follows three childhood friends, now in their late 30s, who reunite after receiving news about the death of the estranged fourth member of their group.

The series blends mystery and comedy in true McGee fashion, as the women embark on "the most thrilling adventure of their lives" through Ireland and beyond, piecing together the truth of their shared past.

"A show about friendship, memory and what happens when life doesn't turn out quite like you'd expected," reads the official description.

**Cast:** Sinead Keenan, Caoilfhionn Dunne, Roisin Gallagher

**Creator:** Lisa McGee (Derry Girls)

**Premiere Date:** February 12, 2026 (Netflix)
