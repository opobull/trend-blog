## Pricing: Simple vs Tiered

### Apple TV+: $6.99/month (One Price, All Features)

**What you get:**
- ‚úÖ All content in 4K HDR
- ‚úÖ Dolby Vision & Dolby Atmos
- ‚úÖ Up to 6 simultaneous streams
- ‚úÖ No ads ever
- ‚úÖ Family Sharing included

---

### Netflix: $6.99 - $24.99/month (3 Tiers)

#### Standard with Ads - $6.99/month
- 1080p (Full HD)
- 2 devices at once
- Frequent ad interruptions
- Some content unavailable

#### Standard - $15.49/month
- 1080p (Full HD)
- 2 devices at once
- No ads
- All content available

#### Premium - $24.99/month
- 4K Ultra HD + HDR
- 4 devices at once

- No ads

**Verdict**: Confusing tiers. You need Premium ($24.99) to match what Apple TV+ offers at $6.99.

## Video & Audio Quality: Apple Dominates

### Apple TV+
- **Always 4K HDR** (when available) - included in base price
- **Dolby Vision** support
- **Dolby Atmos** spatial audio
- **Higher bitrate** - typically 2x Netflix's bitrate
- Result: **Visibly better picture quality**, especially on large screens

### Netflix
- **4K HDR only on Premium tier** ($24.99/month)
- Lower tiers max out at 1080p
- **Lower bitrate** compared to Apple TV+ even at 4K
- Premium features locked behind $25 paywall

**Verdict**: If you have a 4K TV, Apple TV+ looks noticeably better at 1/3 the price of Netflix Premium.

## The Smart Strategy: Rotate Subscriptions

Here's what savvy streamers do in 2026:

### Subscribe to Apple TV+ continuously ($6.99/month)
- Affordable enough to keep year-round
- New quality content monthly
- Best value per dollar

### Rotate Netflix ($15.49 or $24.99 for 1-2 months)
- Subscribe when a big show drops (Stranger Things, Bridgerton, Squid Game)
- Binge for a month
- Cancel until next big release
- **Save $150-250/year** vs keeping it year-round

### Result:
- Always have Apple TV+ for quality
- Get Netflix content without paying for dead months
- Total annual cost: **~$100-150 vs $300+**

## FAQ

### Can I watch Apple TV+ on non-Apple devices?

**Yes!** Apple TV+ works on:
- Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony, Vizio)
- Roku, Fire TV, Chromecast
- PlayStation, Xbox
- Web browsers (tv.apple.com)

### Does Netflix have a free trial in 2026?

**No.** Netflix discontinued free trials in 2020 and has not brought them back.

### Which has better original shows?

**Apple TV+** has higher average ratings per show, but **Netflix** has more total hits due to sheer volume. Quality vs quantity.

### Can I share accounts?

- **Apple TV+**: Yes, Family Sharing up to 6 people
- **Netflix**: Officially no longer allows password sharing outside household (as of 2023)

### Which service is better for 4K content?

**Apple TV+** - all content in 4K included. Netflix requires Premium ($24.99) for 4K.
