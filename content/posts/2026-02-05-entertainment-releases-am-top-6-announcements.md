## 1. The Lincoln Lawyer Season 4 Premieres Today on Netflix

![The Lincoln Lawyer Season 4](/images/entertainment-am-2026-02-05-1.jpg)

**The Lincoln Lawyer Season 4** officially premieres today, February 5, 2026, on Netflix. All 10 episodes are available to stream now. The tables have turned completely on Mickey Haller — this season, the defense attorney becomes the defendant.

Season 4 adapts Michael Connelly's sixth book in the series, "The Law of Innocence." Following the cliffhanger ending of Season 3, Haller was pulled over by police who discovered the body of his former client, Sam Scales, in the trunk of his Lincoln. Now Mickey must defend himself against murder charges while incarcerated.

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Premiere | February 5, 2026 |
| Episodes | 10 (all at once) |
| Platform | Netflix |
| Source Material | The Law of Innocence (2020) |

**Cast:** Manuel Garcia-Rulfo, Becki Newton, Jazz Raycole, Angus Sampson, Neve Campbell (returning as series regular), Elliott Gould, Constance Zimmer as "Death Row Dana," Cobie Smulders, Jason O'Mara, Sasha Alexander

Netflix has also confirmed **Season 5** is already in development, set to begin filming in March 2026 and will adapt "Resurrection Walk."

## 2. Outcome - Apple TV Film with Keanu Reeves, Cameron Diaz, Matt Bomer, and Jonah Hill

![Outcome Apple TV Film](/images/entertainment-am-2026-02-05-2.jpg)

At Apple TV's Press Day on February 3, director and co-star **Jonah Hill** announced that **Outcome** will premiere globally on Apple TV on **April 10, 2026**. This marks Hill's return to Hollywood after a significant absence.

The dark comedy centers on Reef Hawk (Keanu Reeves), a beloved Hollywood star who must dive into the depths of his hidden demons after being extorted with a mysterious video that threatens to shatter his image and career. With the support of his lifelong friends Kyle (Cameron Diaz) and Xander (Matt Bomer), along with crisis lawyer Ira (Jonah Hill), Reef embarks on a soul-searching journey to identify the blackmailer.

**Key Information:**

| Premiere | April 10, 2026 |
| Platform | Apple TV+ |

**Cast:** Keanu Reeves, Cameron Diaz, Matt Bomer, Jonah Hill, Martin Scorsese, Susan Lucci, Laverne Cox, David Spade, Atsuko Okatsuka, Roy Wood Jr., Kaia Gerber

## 3. Imperfect Women - Elisabeth Moss, Kerry Washington, and Kate Mara Star in Apple TV Thriller

![Imperfect Women Apple TV](/images/entertainment-am-2026-02-05-3.jpg)

Apple TV unveiled a teaser trailer for **Imperfect Women**, a psychological thriller premiering **March 18, 2026**. The limited series stars and is executive produced by Elisabeth Moss and Kerry Washington.

Based on Araminta Hall's novel, "Imperfect Women" examines a crime that shatters the lives of three women in a decades-long friendship. The unconventional thriller explores guilt and retribution, love and betrayal, and the compromises that irrevocably alter lives. As the investigation unravels, so does the truth about how even the closest friendships may not be what they seem.

## 4. Monarch: Legacy of Monsters Season 2 - Official Trailer Released

![Monarch Legacy of Monsters Season 2](/images/entertainment-am-2026-02-05-4.jpg)

Apple TV unveiled the official trailer for **Monarch: Legacy of Monsters Season 2** at their Press Day event. The 10-episode second season will premiere globally on **February 27, 2026**, with new episodes releasing every Friday until May 1.

Season 2 picks up with the fate of Monarch — and the world — hanging in the balance. The dramatic saga reveals buried secrets that reunite heroes (and villains) on Kong's Skull Island, and introduces a new, mysterious village where a mythical Titan rises from the sea. ## 5. Eternity - Elizabeth Olsen, Miles Teller in Afterlife Romance Coming February 13

![Eternity Apple TV Film](/images/entertainment-am-2026-02-05-5.jpg)

**Eternity**, an Apple Original Film in association with A24, will premiere on Apple TV on **February 13, 2026** — just in time for Valentine's Day. The film is already Rotten Tomatoes "Certified Fresh."

The romantic comedy from director David Freyne imagines an afterlife where souls have one week to decide where to spend eternity. Joan (Elizabeth Olsen) faces an impossible choice between the man she spent her life with, Larry (Miles Teller), and her first love, Luke (Callum Turner), who died young and has waited decades for her to arrive.

## 6. Margo's Got Money Troubles - Elle Fanning, Michelle Pfeiffer, Nicole Kidman in April

![Margo's Got Money Troubles Apple TV](/images/entertainment-am-2026-02-05-6.jpg)

Apple TV released the teaser trailer for **Margo's Got Money Troubles**, a comedic family drama premiering **April 15, 2026**. The series is created by David E. Kelley and boasts an impressive ensemble cast.

Based on Rufi Thorpe's novel, the series follows Margo (Elle Fanning), a college dropout and Hooters waitress who finds herself pregnant and facing mounting bills. When her estranged father, a former professional wrestler named Jinx (Nick Offerman), moves in, Margo makes creative decisions about supporting her family — including starting an OnlyFans account.
