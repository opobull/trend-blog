## 1. Steven Spielberg's "The Dinosaurs" Drops Trailer with Morgan Freeman Narration

![Entertainment Early Morning Roundup](/images/entertainment-early-2026-02-07.jpg)

**Netflix** has unveiled the official trailer for **The Dinosaurs**, a new documentary series from executive producer **Steven Spielberg** and **Amblin Entertainment**. Academy Award-winner **Morgan Freeman** serves as narrator for this four-episode journey through 170 million years of prehistoric history.

The series promises to deliver the story of the rise and fall of the dinosaurs—where they came from, why they mattered, how they evolved, and how they met their ultimate fate.

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Platform | Netflix |
| Premiere Date | March 6, 2026 |
| Episodes | 4 |
| Narrator | Morgan Freeman |
| Executive Producer | Steven Spielberg |
| VFX Studio | Industrial Light & Magic (ILM) |
| Composer | Lorne Balfe |
| Director | Nick Shoolingin-Jordan |

The production team behind *Life on Our Planet* collaborated with Amblin Entertainment to create photorealistic visuals using ILM's award-winning technology. The sweeping cinematic score by Lorne Balfe complements Freeman's iconic narration.

Directed by **Patrick Hughes** (*The Expendables 3*, *The Hitman's Bodyguard*), the film features Ritchson as the leader of a squad that must confront an unimaginable alien enemy during what should have been a routine training mission.

## 3. "Scream 7" New Featurette: Neve Campbell Reflects on Sidney Prescott's Journey

Written and directed by franchise creator **Kevin Williamson** (his directorial debut in the series), *Scream 7* follows Sidney as she protects her daughter Tatum from a new Ghostface killer in the quiet town of Pine Grove, Indiana.

## 4. Apple TV+ Unveils "Imperfect Women" Teaser with Elisabeth Moss & Kerry Washington

**Apple TV+** has debuted the teaser trailer for **Imperfect Women**, a psychological thriller limited series starring **Elisabeth Moss** and **Kerry Washington**. Based on **Araminta Hall's** bestselling novel, the eight-episode series explores the aftermath of a life-altering crime that exposes deep-seated secrets.

Created by **Annie Weisman** (*Physical*), the series follows three women whose lives intersect after a tragedy that forces them to confront uncomfortable truths about friendship, loyalty, and the facades people maintain.

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Platform | Apple TV+ |
| Premiere Date | March 18, 2026 |
| Episodes | 8 |
| Schedule | Two episodes premiere, then weekly through April 29 |
| Creator/Showrunner | Annie Weisman |
| Studio | 20th Television / Apple Studios |

**Cast:** Elisabeth Moss, Kerry Washington, Kate Mara, Anthony Ramos, Corey Stoll, Simone Ashley

The series is co-produced by Love & Squalor Pictures (Moss and Lindsey McManus's production company), which initially optioned the book. Filming took place in Los Angeles following the California wildfires, which cast member Kerry Washington described as "meaningful" to the production.

## 5. "Widow's Bay" Horror Comedy Sets April 29 Premiere on Apple TV+

**Apple TV+** has announced that **Widow's Bay**, a new horror comedy series, will premiere on **April 29, 2026**. Starring **Matthew Rhys** (*The Americans*), the show is set on a remote New England island where a mayor's attempts at modernization stir up old legends and local superstitions.

The series filmed across New England in 2025, with locations including Worcester, Essex, Gloucester, and Rockport in Massachusetts. The first three episodes will debut together, with subsequent episodes releasing weekly.

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Platform | Apple TV+ |
| Premiere Date | April 29, 2026 |
| Lead | Matthew Rhys |
| Genre | Horror Comedy |
| Filming Locations | Massachusetts (Worcester, Essex, Gloucester, Rockport) |
| Format | First 3 episodes, then weekly |

The series arrives during a relatively quiet year for Stephen King adaptations, with only *The Institute* Season 2 and Amazon's *Carrie* miniseries on the 2026 slate. *Widow's Bay* fills the supernatural horror niche for Apple TV+ subscribers.

## 6. "Monarch: Legacy of Monsters" Season 2 Roars Back February 27 on Apple TV+

Season 2 takes the action to **Skull Island** while introducing a mysterious new threat: **Titan X**, which has been teased in promotional materials. The season promises a "battle of eras" as the characters uncover more about Monarch's hidden history.

**Key Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Platform | Apple TV+ |
| Premiere Date | February 27, 2026 |
| Episodes | 10 (weekly through May 1) |
| Focus | Skull Island, Titan X |
| Franchise | Monsterverse |

**Returning Cast:** Kurt Russell, Wyatt Russell, Anna Sawai, Kiersey Clemons, Mari Yamamoto

The first season became a global streaming hit on Apple TV+, setting high expectations for the sophomore outing. New footage from the trailer showcases expanded Titan encounters and deeper dives into Kong's island origins.
