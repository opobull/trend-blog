## What Happened

Aaron Taylor-Johnson and his wife Sam Taylor-Johnson attended Paris Haute Couture Week on Tuesday, January 27, 2026. The couple was spotted at the Saint Laurent Menswear Fall/Winter 2026-2027 show, where they posed for photographs together.

The following day, on January 28, the couple was seen checking out of their Paris hotel and heading to the airport.

## Key Details

### At the Saint Laurent Show

Aaron Taylor-Johnson, 35, wore a brown leather-look jacket and suit for the event. Sam Taylor-Johnson, 58, opted for a deep green shiny coat. Sam wore her long blonde hair down and accessorized with dark sunglasses, while Aaron sported his longer hair in curls and wore an eye-catching watch.

The couple stayed close throughout the event, cuddling as they posed for photographers.

### Departing Paris

When checking out of their hotel on January 28, Aaron wore a black jacket over a white T-shirt with gray pants. Sam wore a black leather jacket, leopard-print scarf, and black jeans.
