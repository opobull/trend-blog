- **Episodes**: Season 2 currently releasing (Thursday drops)
- **Best for**: Medical drama fans, anyone who loved ER or Grey's Anatomy's better seasons

### 3. Heated Rivalry (2025)

The Canadian import that has become Max's most talked-about new drama. **Heated Rivalry** is an erotic drama about two rival hockey players ‚Äî one American, one Canadian ‚Äî whose competitive relationship conceals a secret affair that unfolds across years of their careers.

### 4. Industry (Season 4, 2025)

**Industry** follows young bankers navigating the brutal, amoral world of a London investment bank. Season 4 continues raising the stakes as Pierpoint & Co. faces its most volatile market conditions yet, while its employees continue their pathological pursuit of success at any cost.
